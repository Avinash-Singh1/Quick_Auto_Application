Frequently Asked Questions

What is the process for booking an auto?

You can book an auto through our app by selecting your source and destination, then choosing your travel date and time.

How can I cancel my booking?

To cancel a booking, go to your bookings history and select the booking you wish to cancel.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, debit cards, and mobile wallets.

Can I book an auto for someone else?

Yes, you can book an auto for someone else by entering their details during the booking process.
